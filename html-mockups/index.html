Audio Visualizer Mockups

Available Audio Parameters

Each mockup can be controlled by these audio dimensions extracted from the Web Audio API:

amplitude
Overall volume level (0-1)
bass
Low frequency energy (20-250 Hz)
mid
Mid frequency energy (250-4000 Hz)
treble
High frequency energy (4000-20000 Hz)
pitch
Dominant frequency detected
spectrum
Full frequency array (128 bins)
